RAMGHAT

Ram Ghat, located near the Harsiddhi Temple in Ujjain is famous for its Kumbh Mela. Kumbh Mela is held in every 12 years. Ram Ghat is the most ancient bathing ghat in connection with the Kumbh celebrations.
