TRIVENI GHAT

Shree Pracheen Navgrah Mandir (The temple of Navagraha), the nine planets, on the Triveni ghat of Kshipra is a prominent centre of attraction for the pilgrims. There is a confluence of Khan river near Triveni ghat on the Kshipra. Mythological sanctity of the invisible river Saraswati, associated with the story of Triveni-sangam, is attributed to this place also.
